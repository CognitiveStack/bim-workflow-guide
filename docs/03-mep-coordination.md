# 03 — MEP Coordination

> **Status: coming soon (Phase 2).**

Coordination focused on **Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing** (and fire protection) systems — the most conflict-prone parts of a building.

This document will follow the standard topic template:
- **What it means** — coordinating ducts, pipes, cable trays, and sprinklers.
- **Why it matters** — MEP systems compete for tight ceiling/shaft space.
- **Where it fits** — a focused part of overall coordination.
- **Typical tools** — Revit, Navisworks, Forma Model Coordination.
- **Simple example** — routing a duct around a beam in the fire station.
